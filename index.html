Hi, I'm Aysha Khan.

I’m a journalist and web producer living in Denver. My work focuses on underrepresented communities—particularly Muslims and South Asians in America— digital culture and the intersections therein. That could mean anything from online harassment to violent sectarianism to a cool new startup.

These days I work as social media editor at the Religion News Service, where I also contribute some freelance reporting on Muslim issues. You may have caught my reporting at ThinkProgress, Technical.ly and other places on the web, too.
