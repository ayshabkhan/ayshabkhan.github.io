Hi, I'm Aysha Khan.

I’m a journalist based in Boston. My work focuses on digital culture, religion and underrepresented communities — particularly Muslims in America.

These days I work as social media editor at the Religion News Service, where I also report on Muslim issues and produce our daily newsletter. Before that, I worked with ThinkProgress, American Journalism Review and the Journalism Center on Children and Families. I'm a 2018 emerging journalists fellow with the Journalism & Women Symposium and a judge for the prestigious Goldziher Prize for journalists covering Muslims in America.

Get my newsletter ¬

"Creeping Sharia" is a newsletter for and about Muslims in America. You should sign up for it. Hooray for more things in your inbox!

Sent out twice a month, each edition contains a roundup of the best links, with an emphasis on original reporting and providing multiple perspectives on issues. We going to go beyond a list of the latest hate crimes to instead offer you some food for thought. I also feature interesting scholars and resources to learn from.
