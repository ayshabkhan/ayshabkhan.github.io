How can I help you?

Let's build something cool together! I'm available for freelance projects in the digital, design and media space. Email me and we'll get the ball rolling. Please note that I'm not accepting any more pro bono projects at the moment.

Oh, and as a little bonus: if you've worked with me as a writer or editor, I offer my personal website-building services at a discounted rate.

Writing + Reporting

Having written for some of the world's biggest news outlets, I can write everything from spot news stories for wire services to longform features for alumni magazines. My usual beats are minorities, religion, South Asian diasporas, tech and digital culture. Hire me to write for your media organization, nonprofit or business.

Check out my reporting at THINKPROGRESS, AMERICAN JOURNALISM REVIEW and NBC.

Editing

Want concise, vibrant writing that packs a punch? Let me help get there. Hire me to add life to your bio, essays and articles, grant applications, marketing materials, web copy and more. I offer light editing and proofreading (for grammar, punctuation and style) or heavier editing (for accuracy, structure and flow).

Read some essays I've edited for The Tempest: "'ARE YOU SURE YOU'RE NOT HAWAIIAN?' THEY ASK" and "DON'T LET KASHMIR'S MEDIA BLACKOUTS BLIND YOU".

Need help building or refreshing your personal website? I can help you with your site's branding and design elements as well as web copy. Take a look at some client sites: MUSLIM MARINE, ABDUL LATIF BENNET and SHUKRI KHAN ART.
